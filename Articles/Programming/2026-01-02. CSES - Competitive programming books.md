written by [Antti Laaksonen](https://cs.helsinki.fi/u/ahslaaks/)

| ![](https://cses.fi/book/cphb.png) | *Competitive Programmer's Handbook* is a free online book whose purpose is to give the reader a thorough introduction to competitive programming. You can download the book [here](https://cses.fi/book.pdf) (PDF) and view the project on [GitHub](https://github.com/pllk/cphb/). |
| --- | --- |
| ![](https://cses.fi/book/gtcp.jpg) | *Guide to Competitive Programming* is a printed book, published by Springer. It is based on *Competitive Programmer's Handbook*, but also discusses more advanced topics. You can buy the book, for example, through [Springer](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-031-61794-2) or [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Guide-Competitive-Programming-Algorithms-Undergraduate-dp-3031617932/dp/3031617932/). |
| ![](https://cses.fi/book/dmkbook.jpg) | *Олимпиадное программирование* (*Olympiad Programming*) is a Russian version of *Guide to Competitive Programming*, published by DMK Press. You can find more information about the book on their [website](https://dmkpress.com/catalog/computer/programming/978-5-97060-644-5/). |
| ![](https://cses.fi/book/gtcp_korean.jpg) | 알고리즘 트레이닝: 프로그래밍 대회 입문 가이드 is a Korean version of *Guide to Competitive Programming*, published by Insight Book. You can find more information about the book on their [website](https://blog.insightbook.co.kr/2019/05/07/%E3%80%8A%EC%95%8C%EA%B3%A0%EB%A6%AC%EC%A6%98-%ED%8A%B8%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%EB%8B%9D-%ED%94%84%EB%A1%9C%EA%B7%B8%EB%9E%98%EB%B0%8D-%EB%8C%80%ED%9A%8C-%EC%9E%85%EB%AC%B8-%EA%B0%80%EC%9D%B4%EB%93%9C/). |
