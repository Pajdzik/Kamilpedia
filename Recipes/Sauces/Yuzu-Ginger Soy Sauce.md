
# Yield
About 2 cups

# Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup seasoned soy sauce (recipe attached)
- 1/4 cup yuzu juice, can substitute lemon juice
- 1/2 cup mirin
- 3 tablespoons ginger, fine julienne

# Steps

1. Mix all ingredients into a bowl.
